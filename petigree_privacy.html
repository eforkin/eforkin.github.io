Petigree Privacy Policy

Last revised on June 20, 2018

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains what information the Petigree Applications ("App") collects and how it is used. By downloading and installing the App, you agree that we (the creator of the App) can collect, transfer, store, and use the information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you have any questions about the Privacy Policy of the App, please contact eforkin@umich.edu.

We reserve the right, in our sole discretion, to make changes or modifications to this Privacy Policy at any time and for any reason. We will alert you about any changes by updating the "Last updated" date of this Privacy Policy, and you waive any right to receive specific notice of each such change. It is your responsibility to periodically review the Privacy Policy to stay informed of updates. You will be subject to, and will be deemed to have been made aware of and to have accepted, the changes in any revised Privacy Policy by your continued use of the App after the date such revised Privacy Policy is posted.

Type of Data Collected

This App collects the user's email address in order to create an account.

Users are responsible for any third-party personal data they obtain, publish, or share through the App. Additionally, users confirm that they have the third party's consent to provide this data to the App. Personal Data may be freely provided by the user, or, in case of Usage Data, collected automatically when using this Application. If the App states, that certain information is not necessasry, the user reserves the right to share or withold this information.

Data Storage

User Data collected from this App is stored using Google's Firebase. To read about how Google secures and processes user data through Firebase, click here. Personal User Data shall be processed and stored for as long as required by the purpose for which they have been collected.

Facebook Permissions asked by the App

In creating a Petigree account with the user's Facebook account, the App may ask for permissions to retrieve user information through Facebook. These permissions are:

  1. Basic information. This default behavior includes the user's Facebook id, name, picture, gender, and locale.
  2. Email

For more information about these permissions, refer to the Facebook privacy policy.

User Rights

User have the right to withdraw their consent to the processing of their data, access their data, verify the accuracy of their data, have their personal data deleted from the App, and lodge a complaint at any time. If you wish to do any of these, please contact eforkin@umich.edu.
